Currently, we only support tab- or comma-separated series like this: 

Federal,  State,  Total
  1.2,     0.8,    2.1
  1.6,     0.7,    2.4
  3.4,     0.5,    3.9
 10.5,     0.5,   11.0
 10.1,     0.4,   10.6
 10.8,     0.4,   11.3
  9.1,     0.5,    9.6
  9.8,     0.5,   10.3
  8.6,     0.5,    9.1
  7.4,     0.6,    8.1
  7.6,     0.6,    8.2

Try coping and pasting it! Then, since this particular bit of data starts in the year 1940, enter “1940” in the “X Value of First Data Point” field and then click “Save and Quit” to get your chart. You’re done!

If you get an error message, it’s probably because of non-numbers like “Q2” or “%.” But your currently-unsupported data will be logged and we’ll email you when we do support it!

We know this is a bit clumsy and confusing right now—thank you for your patience. We’re working on making it completely painless. If you’re stuck or have suggestions, email us at us at help@datacollective.org.

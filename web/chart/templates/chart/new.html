Then:

  • click the button just above that says “2. Click here”.
  • That'll take you to the Edit page, where you'll see the data in living color
  • From there you can add a Title and a Description to the chart
  • When you're happy, hit “Save and Done” to get your chart
  • Voila! Your chart now has its own URL
  • You can click on your chart's Share menu to get an embed code
  • Paste the embed code into any web page or blog
  • Boom! Your chart is on the web.

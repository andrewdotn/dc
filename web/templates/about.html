USE OUR CHART MAKING TOOL


Charts on the web today are just pictures.

Data Collective charts are better, because they're:

  • Interactive, letting your readers inspect individual data points with their mouse
  • Instantly shareable, increasing your readership
  • Linked to their underlying data, which gives you authority. It shows you're not afraid to cite your sources and let people check your work. (Also, this makes the internet better, creating links to meaningful data, which is our hidden agenda)

MISSION

Data Collective is a startup non-profit. We build and support technology to help people find, understand, and share data, advancing the knowledge and education of citizens everywhere.

We started Data Collective because it's way too hard to get simple numerical facts about the world. In sports or business, you could never understand what's going on without the basic stats. We feel the same way about the rest of life.

We are non-profit and strive to be non-partisan and unbiased.
